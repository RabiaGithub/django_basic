    About AIRC

    aIRC(All India Research Championship) is an initiative by TechieNest Pvt. Ltd. to provide a platform to the students across the nation to showcase their talent and empower their technical skills. The mission of organizing aIRC is to inspire students to be technology leaders and innovators, engaging them in exciting tasks which builds up their research & development skills and foster all-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence, communication and leadership. Predominantly, we have successfully associated with esteemed institutes including Techkriti’18 at IIT Kanpur, nVision'17 at IIT Hyderabad and NIT Raipur as our event partners for aIRC'18, aIRC'17 and aIRC'16. Keeping at par with the latest technologies TechieNest in association with AAVRITI’19 at IIT Bombay has come up with aIRC’19 with two revolutionizing themes of Artificial Intelligence and Internet of Things.

    Where to Begin

    Now that you know what's aIRC’19, time to look at all the Stages, Competition Guidelines, your Problem Statements and what REWARDS we have in the house as a token of appreciation for the Winners.

    Stages For aIRC'19

    First is to get through the synopsis submission of projects. Second is clearning Zonals to be held in different colleges, and then you get Ticket to IIT Bombay.

    Process Guidelines

    The project should be based on any of our four stated themes. Students qualifying the synopsis submission and Zonal rounds are elegible for FINALE

    Problem Statements

    In aIRC’19, teams have to submit an idea (problem and solution of that particular problem) based on any or all of our four themes. These ideas should cover a particular field that can reduce human efforts

    Accolades

    Prizes and rewards worth upto 1 Lakh.

    Scholarship for all the finalists in TechieNest SITP(Summer Industrial Training Program) 2019.
